Video Attributes Summary

controls — shows play/pause/volume/fullscreen buttons

width — width of the video player in pixels

height — height of the video player in pixels

autoplay — plays automatically (needs muted in most browsers)

loop — repeats when finished

muted — starts with no sound

poster — thumbnail image shown before video plays
