div tag (no semantic meaning)

Use div when none of the semantic tags fit your content.

Use it just to group things for layout/styling purposes.

Quick Guide: article vs section vs div

article → self-contained content (blog post, comment, product card)

section → chapter/theme of a page that has a heading

div → just a layout box, no semantic meaning
